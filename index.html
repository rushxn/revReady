THE PROBLEM
Riders have been making do
with the wrong tools.
"Where should we ride this weekend?" means googling, checking forum posts from 2019, and hoping the park is actually open.
That weird noise your bike is making means posting on ThumperTalk and waiting 3 days for someone to say "sounds like a valve."
Tracking when you last changed your oil means a sticky note on the garage wall. Or just guessing. Usually guessing.
WHAT'S INSIDE
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.
Trail Finder
Discover OHV parks near your current location — real photos, ratings, difficulty, fees, and one-tap directions.
AI Bike Scan
Point your camera at your bike. Claude AI inspects it, scores the health, and tells you exactly what needs attention.
Bike Health
Maintenance tracker, live OBD2 sensors, and symptom diagnosis. Know your bike's status at a glance, every day.
Ride Log
Log every session — park, duration, engine hours, and a rating. Your riding history, always with you.
Jetting Calculator
Dial in your carb for any altitude and temperature. Built-in specs for every bike in your garage.
Audio Diagnosis
Record your engine. RevReady analyzes the sound signature and flags anomalies before they become breakdowns.

HOW IT WORKS
Open the app.
Go ride.
01
Tell us your bike
Select your brand, model, and year during onboarding. RevReady loads your bike's service intervals, jetting specs, and pulls a photo automatically.
02
Find a trail near you
Trails tab opens straight to the nearest OHV park with a real photo. Filter by distance, difficulty, riding style, or whether it's free.
03
Run a health check before you go
Point your camera at the bike for a 30-second AI inspection. Get a health score and a clear list of what's good, what to watch, and what to fix.
04
Log the ride when you're back
Add engine hours, rate the session, note the park. Your maintenance tracker updates automatically so you never miss a service interval again.
